Hatsune Miku is a vocaloid software voicebank created by Crypton Future Media. She's the official mascot for this company and is 16 years old.
                                Miku has been active since 2007. She is known as one of the most popular virtual idol and is the most recognizable vocaloid out of all.
She's known as a virtual idol and her voice is modeled by Saki Fujita a voice actress. She was the second Voicaloid sold using the Vocaloid 2 engine and
the first japanese version of the 2 engine and the seventh Vocaloid overall. She was released in Augest 2007 and from then on she became one of the most known Vocaloid virtual idol.
