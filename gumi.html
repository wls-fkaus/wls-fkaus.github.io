GUMI was the second VOCALOID released by Internet Co., Ltd. She's a Japanese Voicaloid and was released in June 2009.
                                Gumi has 18 different voicebanks in total which also makes her currently the Vocaloid with the most voicebanks. Gumi wasn't promoted as much as Miku was but still turned out to be popular.
Her english version tends to be quite popular because it was the first English capable vocal to be ablle to purchase from Vocaloid shop.
She's considered as underrated by most public since it feels like Miku "overshadow" Gumi.
